Twitter downloader for the same whose AVD I worked on. This app also has all the functionalities as AVD but is specific for twitter and can download videos from twitter with a little exception that it has an Built in Black and white Themes and Built in directory selector.

  • Built in video player.
  • Up to Android 11 support.
  • Can download mp4 videos from Twitter
  • Multi resolution video download
  • Feedback with Firebase.
  • Cloud In-app messaging
  • In-app Subscription
  • Google Ads
  • Facebook Ads
  • In-app Browse and download
  • Dark and white Theme
  • Download Multiple Files at the same time with download pause resume functionality.
